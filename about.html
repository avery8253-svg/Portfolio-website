About me

I'm a designer shaped by contemporary media and the aesthetics that defined my formative years. What drives me isn't just making something that works — it's creating visuals that feel alive, personal, and worth looking at. I love taking an idea and pushing it, distorting it, refining it until it feels fluid and intentional in the digital space.

In a world saturated with safe, corporate design built for mass appeal, I'm more interested in making work that feels visceral and real — something with presence, not just polish.

what i do

Album artwork, merch graphics, event posters, logo design, CD packaging, and social media assets. If it needs a visual, I can build it.

how i work

I work closely with clients to understand the feel they're after — then push it further. Most projects start with a mood direction and evolve from there. Comfortable with open briefs, and used to working with artists, labels, and independent creators.
